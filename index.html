I started off doing content, copy and marketing writing for tech-staffing firms, real-estate companies and softwear developers.

I really enjoyed the contract work but it wasn't steady enough to stay fully busy with, so I set my sights on technical writing. I obtained my Technical Writing Certificate from California State University in 2020, though I still lacked any real technical experience. I then earned a Technical Support Certificate from Google. Needing to get a deeper understanding and excited by the idea of having career options, I enrolled in the University of Washington Coding Bootcamp for Full-Stack Development to flesh out my education portfolio.
